    GreenDouch’

    A French person can waste between 30 and 60 litres of water a day, with almost 40% of this amount going on personal hygiene. In my opinion, in the near future, this waste could disappear thanks to technological advances. How could this happen? With revolutionary showers that no longer use water, putting an end to this waste.

    The object the shower

    Discover the innovative waterless shower that reinvents hygiene while reducing waste. Clean, green, economical and futuristic.
