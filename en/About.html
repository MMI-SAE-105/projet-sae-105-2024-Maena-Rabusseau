    About

    Hi, my name is Maéna Rabusseau, I'm in my first year of a BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet) in Montbéliard.

    This website is a project on the subject of ‘Prehistory of the Future’. The subject Prehistory of the Future invites us to think about our future. How might current knowledge and technologies be perceived in the distant future? For me, the technologies of the future will provide solutions to the current problems you have just seen.
    If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact me via my social networks below.

    Glossary :  

    Blade Runner : a policeman responsible for eliminating former replicants 
    Replicant : an android, a kind of robot that looks like a human, designed to carry out difficult or dangerous tasks.
