    The shower

    Discover the innovative waterless shower that reinvents hygiene while reducing waste. Clean, green, economical and futuristic.

    The waterless shower: an ecological and economic revolution  

    An innovation that changes our habits while protecting the planet. In a world where water conservation is becoming crucial, a surprising innovation is making its appearance: the waterless shower. This revolutionary technology promises to transform our approach to daily hygiene while combating water wastage.  

    Image of an innovative shower

    "A family of four could save more than 50,000 litres of water a year. "

    Clean, efficient technology

    The waterless shower uses biodegradable micro-particles suspended in an ionised air mist to effectively cleanse the body. This system eliminates impurities and bacteria without using a single drop of water, saving thousands of litres a year.  

    Multiple benefits  

    As well as saving water, this innovative shower reduces the energy used to heat water and eliminates the need for polluting shower products. Although its adoption raises questions, this shower represents a promising step towards a more sustainable future in personal hygiene. This paradigm shift in our approach to daily hygiene represents a giant step towards a sustainable future. At a time when the world is facing unprecedented ecological challenges, this invention could well be the key to a green revolution in our bathrooms, combining economy and ecology in an unprecedented way.

    This frieze shows the gradual evolution of shower standards in the home, from waterintensive systems to increasingly economical and technological solutions.
